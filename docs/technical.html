3.2.4 Add a citation

Citation of a website[^3].

Why? rOpenSci staff and community members who have authored a blog post, tech note, or presented in a Community Call have an author page on our website. Then, when a reader clicks on your by-line in your post, they can find your online home, possibly your Twitter or GitHub handle, and links to all the content youâve authored on our site.
